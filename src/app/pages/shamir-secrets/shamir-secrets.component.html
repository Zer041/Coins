Split into shares area

Here you will be able to split your private key or your public address into different shares

Enter the passphrase in the following forms and split the key. All you need to know is:

     # Shares: are the number of shares you're going to split the key into
     # Theshold: are the number of shares you will need to combine to recover the original keyword












Combine into shares area

Here you will able to combine your pre-shared secrets, you will need to put the number of secrets according to the theshold
